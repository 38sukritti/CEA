Our Story

Rooted in Nature,
Driven by Passion.

Cea. was born from a simple belief – that luxury and nature can coexist beautifully.

We create premium home sprays using high-quality natural ingredients to bring calm, comfort, and elegance into every home.

How It Started

A Passion Project
Turned Purpose.

It started in a small kitchen, experimenting with botanical extracts and essential oils. What began as a personal obsession with creating the perfect home ambiance quickly grew into something much bigger.

We noticed that most home sprays on the market were either full of harsh chemicals or lacked the depth and longevity of a truly premium fragrance. We set out to change that.

Every Cea. formula is the result of months of testing, refining, and perfecting — until it feels exactly right.
